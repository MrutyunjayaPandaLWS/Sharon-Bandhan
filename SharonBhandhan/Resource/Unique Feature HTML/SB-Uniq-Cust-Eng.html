SharonBandhan: UNIQUE FEATURES


  • All members, on successful registration and activation into SharonBandhan, will get 50 Points as Welcome Gift.
  • The points in SharonBandhan never expire. So, you can set the target gift that you want, and you can also set the timeline for the same.
  • As a support to achieve your target, SharonBandhan will add 10% bonus points to your account i.e., you will earn bonus points of 10% on accumulated earned points (unredeemed for 365 days).
  • By default, all members will start at Bronze Level with base points, you will be upgraded to the next levels depending on your points achievement which will have a points multiplier. Refer to the table given below:
Levels Minimum Point Point Multiplier
Bronze Default No multiplier, just base points
Silver On achieving 30,000 points in a year anytime All points get calculated with 5% multiplier
Gold On achieving 60,000 points in a year any time All points get calculated with 10% multiplier
Platinum On achieving 1,20,000 points in a year anytime All points get calculated with 20% multiplier

To retain yourself in the existing level you will have to achieve at least 75% of the minimum points corresponding to the current slab you are, in 12 months.


Tips to maximize:
  1. Enrol to get welcome points.
  2. Participate in a big way to quickly move to higher levels and get base points multipliers.
  3. Keep rolling over your accumulated points over the years to secure another additional 10% bonus and bingo! Your targeted gift will be yours much earlier than you thought.

PROCESS OF EARNING POINTS

Genuine SharonPly product comes with a QR code. Points can be earned by scanning/uploading codes through SharonBandhan App.


Below are the two ways by which you can ensure that earned points are credited to your account: -


  • Scan the QR code through the scanner or manually Upload the code which is on the SharonPly product.
  • Give a missed call to +91 7845858414 (Monday through Friday between 09.30 am and 06.00 pm). Our customer care representative will call back and assist you.

REDEMPTION PROCESS


  • Select the gift you want to redeem from the ‘Redemption Catalogue’. You can select multiple gift items, provided the total points of gifts do not exceed the available earned points.
  • The selected gifts will be added to your cart.
  • Check and confirm the details.
  • After verification, the gifts will be delivered to the registered address (as in the documents uploaded).
    • The confirmed gifts cannot be cancelled or returned.
    • A maximum of 80% of your total earned points are redeemable.
