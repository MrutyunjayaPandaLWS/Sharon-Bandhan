  • All Club members, on successful registration and activation into the club, will get a Welcome 50 Points free, as a token for ShubhMahurat.

PROCESS OF EARNING POINTS:

Each product of CPIL will carry an unique code printed on them. To earn points, each of these unique codes has to be uploaded in the database that will be maintained against each club member:

There are four ways to ensure that points are credited to a club member’s account after purchase :

  • You can scan the QR code printed on products purchased and upload in the App.
  • Give a missed call to 8955177400 (9.30 a.m. to 6 p.m. Monday to Friday) from your registered mobile number. You will receive a call back.You can then share the unique code numbers printed on the products you purchased.
  • You can SMS unique code printed on each product to 9664653344 e.g., CENTURY 12 digit code to 9664653344.
  • From the Sales Executives App: CPIL sales executives will visit all Members at frequent intervals. Members can upload the unique codes through the representative’s APP.

PROCESS TO REDEEM POINTS:

  • You can redeem their points at any time of the program
  • The CenturyProClub redemption form would need to be submitted in the
    • Website or
    • Mobile app
    • Give a missed call to 8955177400
    • (9.30 a.m. to 6 p.m. Monday to Friday) from your registered mobile number. You will receive a call back.
  • CenturyProClub Members can choose any product from a gift catalogue with points less than the current accumulated points of the members. Members can also opt for multiple gifts available for the lower reward point slabs.
    • However once ordered & confirmed, the gifts cannot be cancelled or returned after delivered.
    • Any single redemption of the reward points can be upto a maximum of 80% of the available total points at the time of redemption.
  • Gift will be delivered to your registered address only (as per KYC). The mobile number of member will be used for registering complaints, verification & redemption. Any change in mobile number registered in CenturyProClub, needs to be done via change request form.

SOME QUESTIONS THAT YOU MAY STILL HAVE:

  • Can I buy the product/s from anywhere?
    • Yes, you can purchase products from CPIL distributors/dealers/retailers (but following the existing sales and distribution policy of CPIL)
  • I did not get any points after buying?
    First check if you have followed any one of the four correct ways to get points allocated in your account (refer process of earning points)
    In spite of correct process followed if points do not reflect in your account, you can:
    • Use the ‘submit query ’ feature on the App and lodge your complaint
    • Give a missed call to 8955177400 (9.30 am to 6 p.m Monday to Friday ) from your registered mobile . You will receive a call back.
    • Raise the complaint to CPIL sales Executive
  • CPIL will then check and resolve the issue.
  • I want to know my current point status ?
    • View the dashboard summary on your mobile app
    • You can give a missed call to 8955177400 (9.30 am to 6 p.m Monday to Friday ) from your registered mobile . You will receive a call back.
  • What if I find that the QR code is already used or scanned ?
    • You can use the ‘submit query ’ feature on the App and lodge your complaint./li>
    • You can give a missed call to 8955177400 (9.30 am to 6 p.m Monday to Friday) from their registered mobile .You will receive a call back.
    • Raise the complaint to CPIL sales Executive
    • CPIL will then check and resolve the issue.
  • Can I get cash instead of the gift?
    No, The program as of now allows only redemption against gifts.
  • Can I transfer my points to a fellow member?
    As of now, this is not allowed in the program.
  • What happens if you find gift to be incorrectly redeemed or faulty or you have queries?
    • You can use the ‘submit query ’ feature on the App and lodge your complaint.
    • You can give a missed call to 8955177400 (9.30 am to 6 p.m Monday to Friday ) from their registered mobile. You will receive a call back.
    • Raise the complaint to CPIL sales Executive.
