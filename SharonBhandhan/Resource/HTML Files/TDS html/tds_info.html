• Tax Deduction at Source (TDS) will be applicable on redemption value, once the total amount crosses points worth Rs. 20,000 ( 1 Point = 1 Rupee) :

    - 10% if PAN number available

    - 20% if PAN number not available


  • TDS will be deducted once the redeemed product is delivered to the Member.

  • TDS once deducted will not be reversed and will be paid to the Govt. of India.
