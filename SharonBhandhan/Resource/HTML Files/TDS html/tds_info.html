• Tax Deduction at Source (TDS) will be applicable on redemption value once the total amount cross points worth Rs. 20,000 (1 Point = 1 Rupee):

    - 10% if PAN number available

    - 20% if PAN number not available


  • TDS will be deducted once the redeemed product is delivered to the customer.

  • TDS once deducted will not be reversed, it will be paid to the Govt. of India.
